Info:
~~~~~

Safe and precise transport of workpieces
----------------------------------------

3-axis robot with vacuum gripper works quickly and precisely, positioning workpieces in three-dimensional space. Working range: x-axis 270 degrees, y-axis (forwards/back) 140 mm, z-axis (up/down) 120 mm.

Flow of pieces
--------------

- The station can place a piece down at different places (**YELLOW DOT's**). 
- The station can pick a piece up at different places (**GREEN DOT's**).
